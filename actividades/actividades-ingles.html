Activities for the day on

To begin, we will propose the 3Rs (reduce, reuse, recycle) activity in our daily lives regarding the plastics we use. This means that we should first use as little plastic as possible, such as in the case of plastic bags or bottles..


The following refers to the fact that if we use these bags, bottles, and cups, we should reuse them and not generate more, since these don't completely degrade in two human lifetimes, and this is a danger to the ecosystem, as it damages it by generating this waste in large quantities

Plastics recollection

This activity is based on collecting plastics, primarily on beaches, where most of the plastic is found. The collection would be done by hand, and our volunteers would wear biodegradable gloves and store the trash in bags also made from the same plastic they find, thus fulfilling the 3Rs mentioned above..


Isles of plastics

These large, dense islands form as we dump plastic waste into the water, especially that which comes from drains. We have released 8.3 billion tons of this polymer into circulation globally, according to estimates by the University of California, and the most worrying thing is that more than 70% of it is now waste, clogging up the planet's landfills and oceans.
