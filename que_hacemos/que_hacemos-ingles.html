What we do?

What we do as an NGO is clean beaches and oceans. We are aware of the damage we cause to marine fauna and flora, and marine life in general. That's why we want to do good for the planet as an NGO by cleaning beaches, lakes, rivers, and oceans in general. As a second objective, we want to raise awareness about the damage we cause when we throw waste into the ocean, the importance of reducing plastic use, and how to recycle it.

To this end, we have created both an activities section and a donation section, including a charity shop. In the activities section, we specify what we will be doing and their respective schedules. In the donations section, you can donate money to purchase materials and personnel for beach cleanups. We also mention a section with a charity shop, where the money will be used for the same purposes as the donations, but you can purchase NGO merchandise.


Nuestra visión y misión


Vision

Our main vision is that, in the not-too-distant future, we hope to be able to live in a world without ocean pollution, where both beaches and oceans in general are clean of filth, and where all those islands of accumulated waste drifting aimlessly in the sea disappear completely.

At the same time, we also see a world that stops killing and destroying marine fauna and flora due to the immense amount of waste dumped into the oceans, a world in which the vast majority of people commit to recycling.


Mission

Therefore, our NGO will be responsible for fulfilling that vision with the help of all those volunteers who are involved in cleaning up our seas, starting by gathering people and making ourselves visible on social media so that people join our cause. In the future, we would like to give talks in schools and/or institutions to raise awareness among young people about caring for the environment, not throwing waste into the sea, and, above all, recycling it, all for a better future for everyone.

Finally, we want people to know that this is not just an NGO problem, but a problem for everyone, and that we all have to do our part to achieve it. For this reason, we want people to join us and help us achieve it.
